Hi, I'm Jesse!

I work across the full spectrum of web development and digital marketing—from writing code to designing interfaces and analyzing how people interact with websites. My work spans both front-end and back-end development, along with the marketing systems that connect businesses to their audiences.

Let's Connect

Professional Experience

Web Development

I build and maintain websites using JavaScript, PHP, HTML, and CSS, primarily working with an in-house content management system or Wordpress. My work includes setting up staging sites, implementing UI design, creating custom plugins, and website launches.

Full-Stack Application Development

I’ve been involved in designing and building web applications from front to back. My experience includes extending the functionality of our content management systems, adding features to existing applications, and collaborating with others to build great user experiences. I apply object-oriented programming principles to write maintainable, scalable code.

Web Design

I design complete websites and marketing landing pages from initial concept through final implementation. Working in Figma, I create wireframes and visual designs that work well across all device sizes. I own the entire design process, making strategic decisions that balance user needs with business goals.

Analytics & Tracking

I set up tracking systems that help our team understand how people use our clients’ websites and whether marketing campaigns are working. This includes configuring Google Analytics 4, creating custom reports and dashboards in Looker Studio, and implementing tags that capture important user actions across Google Ads and Facebook Ads.

SEO & GEO

I help websites rank higher in search results and appear in AI-generated answers by optimizing page titles, descriptions, and content structure. This includes implementing structured data that helps search engines understand each page, conducting research to identify the best keywords to target, and performing technical audits to fix issues that might be hurting visibility.

Digital Advertising

I’ve been involved in planning and writing Google Ads campaigns, selecting keywords that connect business with quality leads. My work includes crafting compelling ad headlines and descriptions that drive clicks and conversions.

UX Research

I’ve conducted user interviews to understand how people use software systems, particularly focusing on customer relationship management (CRM) tools. These conversations revealed pain points and opportunities, providing insights that guided product improvements.

Documentation & Technical Writing

I’ve written clear, practical guides that help teams use complex systems effectively. Using frameworks like Jobs-to-Be-Done, I document everything from CMS workflows to analytics setup, ensuring everyone has the information they need to work efficiently.

Notion Systems

I build custom Notion workspaces that help teams stay organized and productive. My systems include social media calendars, project management dashboards, and time tracking tools that make it easy to see what's happening across projects and who's working on what.
